Welcome to Shark Cove!

The game is played as follows.

1. you are the submarine

2. Bubbles either contain powerups, the end of the level or Sharks that will kill you

3. If you approach bubbles that have a shark in them the shark indicator on the top left of the screen will grow.

4. Have fun completing all 10 levels.


To install simply

1. Download xcode.

2. Download this project

3. Hit the play button on the top left of the window!
